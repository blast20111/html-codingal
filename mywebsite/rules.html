⚙️ Basic Rules

🎈Each team has 6 players on the court

🎈A point is scored when the ball touches the opponent’s ground, or they commit a fault (e.g., hitting the ball out, double touch, or failing to return).

🎈Teams are allowed 3 touches before sending the ball back over the net.

🎈Matches are usually played in best of 5 sets. A set is won by reaching 25 points with at least a 2-point lead (final set usually goes to 15 points).

📏 Court & Equipment

🎈Court size: 18m x 9m.

🎈Men: 2.43m, Women: 2.24m

🎈Ball: Lightweight, spherical, and slightly larger than a soccer ball.

🔑 Positions

🎈Setter – playmaker, sets up attackers.

🎈Outside Hitter (Left Spiker) – strong attacker, plays front and back row.

🎈Opposite Hitter (Right Spiker) – attacker opposite setter, often the main scorer.

🎈Middle Blocker – tall players specializing in blocking and quick attacks.

🎈Libero – defensive specialist wearing a different jersey, cannot attack or serve

🎈Defensive Specialist – substitute for back-row defense.
